Welcome to Mohenjo-daro!

Mohenjo-daro, located in the Sindh province of Pakistan, is an ancient archaeological site that dates back over 4,500 years. This UNESCO World Heritage site offers a glimpse into the Indus Valley Civilization, one of the world's oldest urban settlements.

Key Features:

  • Ancient Ruins: Explore the well-preserved ruins of Mohenjo-daro, including the Great Bath, the Granary, and the iconic Dancing Girl sculpture.
  • City Planning: Witness the advanced city planning of Mohenjo-daro, with its well-structured streets, houses, and a complex drainage system.
  • Cultural Significance: Discover the cultural and historical significance of Mohenjo-daro, providing insights into the ancient civilization's social, economic, and religious practices.
  • Museum Visits: Visit the on-site museum that showcases artifacts and relics from Mohenjo-daro, offering a deeper understanding of its history and culture.
  • Archaeological Excavations: Learn about the ongoing archaeological excavations and research activities, which continue to unravel the mysteries of this ancient civilization.
