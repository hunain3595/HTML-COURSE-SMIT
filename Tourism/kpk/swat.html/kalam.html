Welcome to Kalam!

Kalam, nestled in the picturesque Swat Valley of Pakistan, is a breathtaking hill station renowned for its pristine natural beauty, majestic mountains, and glistening waterfalls. Surrounded by lush green forests and alpine meadows, Kalam offers a serene retreat for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Key Features:

  • Scenic Beauty: Immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes of Kalam, with snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and crystal-clear rivers.
  • Mahodand Lake: Visit the mesmerizing Mahodand Lake, located in the upper Ushu Valley, and enjoy boating, fishing, and picnicking in this tranquil setting.
  • Ushu Forest: Explore the enchanting Ushu Forest, a dense forest teeming with wildlife and offering opportunities for nature walks and birdwatching.
  • Matiullah Khan Waterfall: Witness the majestic Matiullah Khan Waterfall, cascading down from the mountains, providing a breathtaking sight and a perfect spot for photography.
  • Trekking and Hiking: Embark on thrilling treks and hikes in the surrounding mountains, such as the scenic trek to the beautiful Jahaz Banda and Kandol Lake.
  • Local Culture: Experience the warm hospitality and rich cultural heritage of the local people, and savor traditional cuisine and handicrafts.

Plan Your Visit:

Ready to experience the heaven on earth that is Kalam? Plan your visit now and immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring beauty of this captivating hill station!
