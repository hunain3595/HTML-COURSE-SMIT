Welcome to Khuzdar!

Khuzdar, a city located in the picturesque province of Balochistan in Pakistan, serves as the gateway to the region and offers a unique blend of natural wonders, cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Surrounded by rugged mountains and fertile valleys, Khuzdar is a hidden gem waiting to be explored.

Key Features:

  • Hanna Lake: Explore the breathtaking beauty of Hanna Lake, a serene reservoir nestled amidst hills, perfect for boating, picnics, and nature walks.
  • Kalat Valley: Discover the scenic Kalat Valley, known for its lush greenery, panoramic views, and historical landmarks such as the Kalat Fort.
  • Moola Chotok: Experience the enchanting oasis of Moola Chotok, a hidden treasure featuring waterfalls, natural pools, and stunning rock formations.
  • Historical Landmarks: Visit the ancient historical sites in Khuzdar, including the intriguing Mehrgarh archaeological site, showcasing remnants of one of the earliest civilizations.
  • Local Handicrafts: Immerse yourself in the local culture by exploring the traditional handicrafts of Khuzdar, such as intricate embroidery, mirror work, and Balochi rugs.
  • Cuisine: Indulge in the delicious Balochi cuisine, known for its rich flavors and unique dishes like Sajji, Landhi, and Balochi Pulao.
