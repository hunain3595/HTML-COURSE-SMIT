Welcome to Naran!

Naran, nestled in the picturesque Kaghan Valley of Pakistan, is a charming mountain town known for its breathtaking natural beauty and serene ambiance. Surrounded by towering peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and lush green meadows, Naran offers a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Key Features:

  • Scenic Landscapes: Immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes of Naran, with majestic mountains, gushing waterfalls, and meandering rivers.
  • Lake Saif-ul-Malook: Visit the enchanting Lake Saif-ul-Malook, famous for its emerald-green waters and fascinating folklore.
  • Babusar Pass: Explore the mesmerizing Babusar Pass, a high-altitude mountain pass that offers panoramic views of the surrounding peaks.
  • Trekking and Hiking: Embark on thrilling treks and hikes in the surrounding valleys, including the beautiful Ansoo Lake and Lalazar Plateau.
  • Outdoor Activities: Engage in a variety of outdoor activities, such as fishing, horse riding, and camping, amidst the pristine natural surroundings.
  • Local Culture: Experience the warm hospitality and rich cultural heritage of the local people, and savor traditional cuisine and handicrafts.
